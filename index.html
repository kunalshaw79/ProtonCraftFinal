About ProtonCraft

Our Name, Our Mission

ProtonCraft combines 'Proton,' the core of a hydrogen atom, with 'Craft,' symbolizing creation. Just as hydrogen's single proton is the building block of the universe, ProtonCraft harnesses this fundamental power to fuel innovation and create new possibilities.

Hydrogen: Key to Decarbonization

Reduction in costs of renewable energy, cost of electrolysers coming down by as much as 60% in the last 3 years, and an overall system efficiency ecosystem getting more ripe has led us to believe in the role of hydrogen to decarbonize our economies.

~90% of required carbon reductions from green hydrogen

Advancing Hydrogen Storage Technology

Hydrogen, with its very high calorific value compared to fossil fuels, is the best alternative in the near future to save this planet. Advancements in technology to store H₂ at high pressure have made commercialization possible by significantly increasing volumetric density - better than Li-ion batteries at more than 100 bar pressure. H₂ is the prominent green energy storage medium.

Problem in Hydrogen Production

Grey Hydrogen

  • 95% of all hydrogen is grey with high emissions
  • Cost: $1-2.1 per kg
  • Produced from steam reforming of natural gas
  • For every 1 kg of H₂, 14 kg of CO₂ released

Green Hydrogen

  • Highly expensive due to high energy consumption
  • Cost: $3.6-5.8 per kg
  • Using purified H₂O with ~65% efficiency
  • Wastage of limited fresh water resources

Industrial Hazard

  • Natural gas mines contain 28% deadly H₂S gas
  • Major sources: Oil refineries, Tannery, Coke-oven
  • Currently, H₂S waste is oxidized
  • Produces unvaluable by-products: Sulfur and Steam

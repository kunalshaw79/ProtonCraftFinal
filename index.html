About ProtonCraft

ProtonCraft combines 'Proton,' the core of a hydrogen atom, with 'Craft,' symbolizing creation. Just as hydrogen's single proton is the building block of the universe, ProtonCraft harnesses this fundamental power to fuel innovation and create new possibilities.

Hydrogen: Key to Decarbonization

Reduction in costs of renewable energy, cost of electrolysers coming down by as much as 60% in the last 3 years and an overall system efficiency- ecosystem getting more ripe and efficient has led us to believing in the role of hydrogen to decarbonise our economies- ~90% of the required carbon reductions are estimated to come from the use of green hydrogen!

Advancing Hydrogen Storage Technology

Abundance H2 having very high calorific value compare to fossils fuels, is the best alternative in near future to save this planet. There were some problem with H2 like low volumetric energy density but advancement in technology to store H2 at high pressure has made the commercialization possible by significantly increasing the Volumetric density better than Li ion batteries at more than 100 bar pressure. So, H2 is the prominent green energy storage medium.

    Problem In Hydrogen Production

    Grey Hydrogen

    • 95% of all is Grey Hydrogen with high emissions costs around $1-2.1
    • Produced from steam reforming of natural gas
    • For every 1 kg of H2, 14 kg of CO2 released

    Green Hydrogen

    • Highly expensive due to high energy consumption cost around $3.6-5.8
    • Using purified H2O with around 65% efficiency.
    • Wastage of fresh water resources which is limited.

    Industrial HAZARD - abundant hydrogen SULFIDE

    • Natural Gas mines contain 28% deadly H2S Gas.
    • Major sources - Oil refineries, Tannery industry, Coke-oven, etc.
    • Currently, H2S waste is oxidized, producing just unvaluable by-products: Sulfur and Steam.

    Our Solution

    Our technology transforms hydrogen sulfide into hydrogen, supporting refineries and producing valuable sulfur for various industries.

    We are converting waste to wealth!!
